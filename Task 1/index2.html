Molecular Formula

It is the formula of a substance which shows the actual number of atoms present in the molecule.

Molecular formula gives more clear information about a compound as it shows the total number of itoms in the compound.
Covalent compounds exists as discrete molecules which are represented by their molecular formula; it provides us more clear information about the compound.

For Example:
C6H6 is the molecular formula of benzene.
C6H12O6 is the molecular formula of glucose and it consists of 6 formula units.

Molecular Mass

It is the sum of atomic masses of all atoms of all the elements present in the molecules.

Molecular Mass is expressed in "amu" relative to atomic mass of C12
For e.g, the molecular mass of one molecule of water is 18 a.m.u. which is 18 times heavier than 1/12th mass of C12 atom.
